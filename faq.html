Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need fasting for investigations?

    Yes, certain investigations do need fasting status. The most common
    investigations which require fasting include :-
    • • Lipid Profile – 12 Hours
    • • Blood Sugar Fasting – 10 to 12 Hours
    • • Insulin Fasting – 10 to 12 Hours
    • • C Peptide Fasting – 8 to 10 Hours
    • • Ultrasound – Whole abdomen, Upper abdomen
    • • TMT
    Common investigations which do not require fasting include:-
    • • Kidney Function Test (Urea, Creatinine, Uric Acid, Electrolytes)
    • • Liver Function Test (Bilirubin Total, Direct, SGOT, SGPT, Alkaline Phosphatase, GGTP)
    • • Thyroid Function Test
    • • HbA1c
    • • Urine Culture
    • • ECG It is always advisable to talk to the lab In-charge and clarify in advance.

  • How do I prepare for an Ultrasound Examination?

    Ultrasound Whole Abdomen : Fasting.

  • What are the timings for Global Diagnostics?

    For blood tests Global Diagnostics is open 6:30 am to 9:30 pm, For timings of other investigations please contact us on 040-40 330088.

  • Do you have home collection facility?

    Yes, home collection facility is available in Kondapur, Manikonda and Suncity, Please note, it is only with prior appointment and is for free of cost.

  • What all tests are available?

    In laboratory we are doing all routine and specialized tests are available. In our diagnostic division, following investigations are available: -

    • ECG
    • Digital X Ray
    • Pulmonary Function Tests
    • TMT
    • Ultrasound
    • Colour Doppler
    • Digital OPG

    It is always advisable to talk to the lab In-charge and clarify in advance.

  • Is there any special provision for senior citizens?

    Yes, we offer a 10% discount on our price list to all patients above 60 years if age. Please note this is not valid on any other panel price list or can not be merged with any other discount.

  • How do I give a urine sample?

    For blood tests Global Diagnostics is open 6:30 am to 9:30 pm, For timings of other investigations please contact us on 040-40 330088.

    • Urine Routine & Microscopic: Any urine in a clean container.
    • Urine Culture & Sensitivity: Preferably first urine, mid-stream, in a sterile culture container.
    • (Available at the lab).
    • Urine Microalbumin: Preferably second urine, in a clean container.
    • 24 Hours Urine: Please collect container from lab and ask for instructions.

  • How and when can I collect my report?

    Reports for blood tests are available on the website and through courier as well on request. Reports for non pathology investigations have to be collected from lab only.

  • How can I book my home collection?

    You can call our home collection line between 6:30 am to 9:30 pm at 9550332837 or reception at 040-40 330088 and book a suitable appointment.

  • What are the various mode of payments available?

    You can by : –

    • Cash
    • Credit Card
    • Cheques

  • Q. Can I make a part payment of requested investigations?

    Yes, the investigations will be processed and reports will be printed but not delivered via courier, or over the internet. Reports with balance payments are not released.
