Careers

Global Diagnostics is a organization built around people with complete dedication towards a common goal of high end patient care and quality. We are an equal opportunity provider providing a challenging, stimulating and professional work environment. We believe in having the best talents having a passion for excellence, a hunger to grow and compassion to care, and encourage them to achieve a higher position not only in our organization, but our society.

Opportunities are available in various disciplines not only for management, pathologists, radiologists, cardiologists, microbiologists, biochemists, doctors, Customer Care, reception, front desk but also we try to train various unskilled personnel and inculcate them in various non technical domains.

Training Programs

Lab Technician: If you have just completed your DMLT or BSc MLT, we can help you learn and gain the right knowledge and skills to give your career a head starts in the field of laboratory medicine. You can work with us as a trainee technician for 3 months, during which we will teach you basic skills of pertinent areas in laboratory and give you an opportunity to observe how work is done in a NABL Accredited laboratory.
